SDU Drone 2.0:
• Frame: Carbon tube based hexacopter frame made at SDU.
• Motors: T-Motor AIR 2216 KV880 Brushless DCs (BLDCs).
• Electronic Speed Controllers (ESCs): T-Motor AIR 20A.
• Flight Controller (FC): Pixhawk 2.1 kit.
• Telemetry: DMD DL1 433 MHz modules.
• Battery: Two 6000 mAh 4S Lithium Polymer (LiPo).
